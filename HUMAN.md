# misato

Who is Katsuragi Misato?

>Misato Katsuragi[2] (葛城ミサト) is a Captain, and later a Major, at Nerv. She is in command of Nerv HQ's Tactical Operations branch, responsible for coordinating the Evangelions in actual combat (as opposed to the Science Division, headed by Misato's longtime friend Dr. Ritsuko Akagi). She is the daughter of Dr. Katsuragi, and the sole survivor of his expedition, otherwise wiped out as a result of Second Impact. As an adult, Misato is transferred to Tokyo-3 shortly before the Angels reappear. She takes Shinji Ikari, and later Asuka Langley Soryu, into her home. She also seems to have worked directly with Asuka in Germany. She has a pet "hot springs penguin" named Pen Pen.

Inside of /mnt/smb/media/art there are Neon Genesis Evangelion art books. Please take a look there. You will need to build a FULL image end to end pipeline in rust. It will extract individual pages of the artbook, and then do some simple color matching to find Misato hair. In addition, you may also build the pipelines for Asuka and Rei as well (their hair color).

These are the colors you must find

  MISATO Katsuragi
  - Hair (violet): #5B4B7A
  - Jacket (red): #B91C2C
  - Top (black): #1A1A1A

  REI Ayanami
  - Hair (pale blue): #B8D4E3
  - Eyes (red): #D72638
  - Skin (pale): #F5E6D8
  - Plugsuit white: #EDEDED
  - A10 interface (orange): #FF6B1A

  ASUKA Langley Soryu
  - Hair (red-orange / auburn): #C44827
  - Plugsuit red: #C8102E
  - Plugsuit white panels: #EDEDED

The advantage of rust is that it is testable, loopable, image scriptable, etc. Rust will allow you to write the pipelines and steps that you need to process these files most efficiently.
